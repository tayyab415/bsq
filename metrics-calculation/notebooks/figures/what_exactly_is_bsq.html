BSQ modules
A shot is scored as six connected phases
The window runs from roughly 2.5 seconds before impact to 1.5 seconds after release. Each phase has its own feature family and confidence gate.
P1Context

Location, angle, pressure, defenders, keeper position.

P2Approach

Run-up, body-ball setup, trunk readiness, timing.

P3Coil

Loading, shoulder-hip separation, balance.

P4Impact

Contact quality, plant base, foot path.

P5Follow

Continuation, post-impact balance, control.

P6Output

Speed, launch, goal-plane projection, placement.
